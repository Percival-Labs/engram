# DeepDive

Thorough multi-source research. Produces a structured brief suitable for decision-making or learning.

## Steps

1. **Decompose** -- Break the topic into 3-5 independent research questions that together cover the topic comprehensively
2. **Search** -- Research each question independently using targeted web searches (2-3 searches per question)
3. **Cross-reference** -- Compare findings across sources. Flag contradictions, note consensus, identify gaps
4. **Synthesize** -- Compile findings into a structured research brief

## Output Format

```markdown
## Research Brief: [Topic]

### Summary
[2-3 sentence executive summary]

### Key Findings
1. [Finding with supporting evidence]
2. [Finding with supporting evidence]
3. [Finding with supporting evidence]
...

### Sources
- [Source 1 title](URL) -- [what it contributed]
- [Source 2 title](URL) -- [what it contributed]
...

### Open Questions
- [Question that could not be fully answered]
- [Area needing further investigation]
```

## Verification

- [ ] At least 3 independent sources consulted
- [ ] Findings include specific evidence, not just assertions
- [ ] Contradictions between sources are noted
- [ ] Open questions are identified honestly
