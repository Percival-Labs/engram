Setup instructions

After downloading, follow the steps for your platform.

Unzip the download

Extract the zip file. You'll see a folder with your AI's files.

Create a new Project

Go to claude.ai, click Projects in the sidebar, then Create Project. Name it after your AI.

Add custom instructions

In the project, click the gear icon. Under Custom Instructions, paste the entire contents of INSTRUCTIONS.md.

Upload knowledge files

Click Add Content and upload: context.md, memory-starter.md, and all files from the skills/ folder.

Start chatting

Open a new conversation in the project. Your AI will greet you by name.

Extract the zip file. Open CHATGPT-INSTRUCTIONS.md.

Open Custom Instructions

Go to chatgpt.com → Settings → Personalization → Custom Instructions.

Paste the "About you" section

Copy the first section from CHATGPT-INSTRUCTIONS.md into "What would you like ChatGPT to know about you?"

Paste the "Response style" section

Copy the second section into "How would you like ChatGPT to respond?"

Start chatting

ChatGPT will now use your AI's personality. For skills, paste individual skill files at the start of conversations when needed.
